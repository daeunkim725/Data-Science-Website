Programs

We engage the Princeton University community with 3 core programs:

Competitions

We train, sponsor and help organize student teams to compete in top data science and machine learning competitions.

Data@Night + Talks

We host data science workshops (Data@Night) or colloquia from guest speakers on a weekly basis.

Networking

We help create bridges between students and industry.
